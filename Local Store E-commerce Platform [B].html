Our Story

NeighborMart was founded in 2010 with the mission to support local producers and provide our community with fresh, high-quality products at fair prices.

What started as a small family store has grown into a community hub where neighbors meet and local businesses thrive. We're proud to source over 80% of our products from within 50 miles of our store.

Now we're bringing that same personal touch and local quality to your doorstep with our new online store.
